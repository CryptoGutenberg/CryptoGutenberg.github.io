Project CryptoGutenberg(PCG) is

similar to Project Gutenberg(PG)
in that it digitizes and archives cultural works.

Blockchain

A permanent records.
PCG stores data on blockchain network differently from PG.
With these efforts,
we can preserve stored culture works
more securely and permanently.

For example, on the Ethereum platform,
data is preserved unless more than half of
14,969 network nodes (as of April 8) are manipulated or deleted.

The Holy Bible

As a first step,
We try to upload the Holy Bible on a block chain.
As the actual Holy Bible was printed by Gutenberg.
The project will proceed sequentially from Genesis to Revelation.
